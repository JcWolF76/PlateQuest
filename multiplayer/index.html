👆 How to Spot a Plate
See a license plate from another state or province? Swipe right on its card in the list below. You earn coins and points — more for rarer plates, plus a bonus if you're the first in the pack to spot it.

🏆 Points & Coins
The number on each card is its rarity value in points. First finder gets full points + 5🪙 bonus coins. Spot all plates in a region to earn extra coin bonuses and unlock badges.
